About Oceano Games

Founded by João, an engineer of sound and aspiring game designer, Oceano Games creates a bridge between the rhythm of the ocean and the fluidity of sound in gameplay. We believe in crafting experiences where sound is not just an accessory, but the core element that drives the game forward.

Our games are built on the foundation of **afro-Brazilian rhythms**, spiritual connection, and dynamic gameplay that ranges from calming and meditative to unpredictable and thrilling. At Oceano Games, we celebrate inclusion, creativity, and adventure.
